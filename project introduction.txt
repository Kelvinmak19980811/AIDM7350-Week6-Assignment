Simple Traffic Simulator
The Simple Traffic Simulator is a Mobile VR application that teaches kids to cross roads safely using game elements; Players must cross roads to complete missions.

2022 Apr - 2022 June
VR Driving School
This project simulates a driving school for learners in Hong Kong. It features a map based on the Ho Man Tin exam route and includes a rule system for exams, along with simple pedestrian and traffic systems.

The application utilizes eye-tracking technology to provide an immersive and realistic experience.

2022 Nov - 2023 Nov
Spatial Project
This research project consists of five mini-games aimed at testing and enhancing spatial skills. It includes a database to track players' reaction times and accuracy.

As the lead developer, I was responsible for all aspects of game content, including programming, user interface design, game design, storytelling, scene design, and database integration.

2023 Apr - 2024 Apr
Smart Traffic Fund - VR Driving School Simulator
This project is an advanced version of the VR Driving School Project, funded by the Smart Traffic Fund. In this enhanced version, a hand-tracking system has been implemented, enabling users to interact with various interior features, such as the hand brake, gear shifter, hazard lights, turn signals, and the engine start button.

The project utilizes a Gaussian splat pipeline to simulate the streets of Hong Kong.
